Lighthouse Resort Orlando  ·  Orlando, Florida

Resort amenities that win the booking and keep guests coming back.

Every facility at Lighthouse Resort has been selected with one question in mind: does this give a family or group a reason to book this property over every alternative on the market? The answer drives occupancy, extends average stay length, and builds the repeat booking rate that stabilizes annual revenue.

01  /  09

Florida sun. Lagoon pool. No queue.

Most guests visiting Orlando need at least one day away from the parks, and this is where they spend it. A lagoon-style centerpiece with sun shelf entry, private cabanas, and room to genuinely relax gives families the Florida pool day they came for, without leaving the resort.

02  /  09

The detail that turns a first stay into a tradition.

Winding through lush tropical landscaping, the lazy river is the amenity that that appears in the booking decision before the guest has even looked at room rates.

For investors, it represents something more specific: a repeat-booking engine built into the asset itself, reducing acquisition cost per guest stay and strengthening long-term occupancy.

03  /  09

Keeps the youngest guests happy and parents relaxed.

Ground jets, tipping buckets, and spray features on a safe, non-slip surface, creating a dedicated zone for younger guests that frees parents to relax on-site rather than manage them at the pool. The amenity that makes a multi-generational booking easy.

04  /  09

Built on a proven foundation.

Lighthouse Resort - Orlando is a hybrid development: part strategic remodel of an established resort property, part new construction. The existing resort is being brought fully up to premium standard, while several new buildings are being added to the site, expanding the resort's footprint and unit count. For investors, this approach offers the best of both: the infrastructure and cost advantages of an existing property, combined with the yield potential of a purpose-designed new development.

05  /  09

On-site dining that keeps revenue in the resort.

An on-site pizzeria with poolside delivery solves the question every family asks during their stay. Wood-fired menu, casual atmosphere, and the convenience that keeps on-property food and beverage spend from walking out the front gate.

06  /  09

An amenity that's becoming a guest expectation.

Pickleball is the fastest-growing recreational sport in the United States, and resort guests are now actively filtering for properties that offer it. Two regulation courts with shade structures and equipment on request. The amenity that gives multi-generational groups a reason to stay active on-property and Lighthouse Resort - Orlando a differentiator in platform search.

07  /  09

A revenue line with a view.

Panoramic Orlando views, signature cocktails, and a format that works three ways: daily guest lounge, private event venue, and dedicated wedding space. Prime views of Disney’s daily firework show.

Each wedding or event booking generates revenue entirely independent of unit occupancy, a meaningful ancillary income stream built into the asset. Between events, the rooftop is open to all guests, creating the elevated social atmosphere that earns the "this place is different" review and drives the direct repeat bookings that reduces platform commission dependency over time.

08  /  09

Built for guests who stay longer than a weekend.

The fitness centers at most resorts were clearly not designed for people who actually use them. The Lighthouse Resort is different, a full-scale facility with commercial-grade equipment, a proper free weights floor, and dedicated space for stretching and functional training, sized and specified for a 600-unit resort. For the growing segment of guests who refuse to skip a workout on vacation, it's the detail that wins the booking.

09  /  09

When the couple says yes, the whole party needs a room.

The Lighthouse Resort's dedicated wedding chapel sits atop the resort with panoramic Orlando views, a purpose-built ceremony space available for exclusive rental. For investors, the significance is not the romance. It's the reservation block that follows every booking.

Wedding parties are among the most consistent group-booking drivers in hospitality. When a couple selects a venue, their guests follow: extended family, the wedding party, and out-of-town friends, filling rooms that would otherwise require platform spend to reach. A single weekend wedding generates chapel rental revenue plus a full resort block at premium weekend rates, converting what is typically a high-demand, hard-to-fill period into a sold-out event.

Built for guests. Structured for investors.

Every amenity at Lighthouse Resort - Orlando was designed to drive occupancy, extend stays, and bring guests back year after year. Speak with the deal team to see how that translates into the financial model.
