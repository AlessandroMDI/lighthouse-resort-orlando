Lighthouse Key Resort  ·  Orlando, Florida

Amenities

Resort-grade facilities designed to make every stay unforgettable — and every owner's returns consistent.

At Lighthouse Key, the amenities aren't an afterthought — they're the reason guests choose this property over every other option on the market. From a resort-style pool complex to a pizza restaurant steps from the door, every facility has been selected to maximise both guest satisfaction and owner occupancy rates.

01  /  08

Resort Pool

A lagoon-style centrepiece that anchors the resort. Generous depths, sun shelf entries, and private cabanas positioned for both shade and spectacle. The pool is where guests linger longest — and where five-star reviews are written.

02  /  08

Lazy River

A winding current that loops through lush tropical landscaping. Guests drift at their own pace past palms and water features. It's the amenity families book around — and the detail that turns a one-time stay into an annual tradition.

03  /  08

Splash Pad

A dedicated water play zone built for the youngest guests. Ground jets, tipping buckets, and spray features on a safe, non-slip surface. Parents relax nearby while children play freely — a dynamic that drives longer stays and stronger reviews.

04  /  08

The Existing Resort

Lighthouse Key is a hybrid development — part strategic remodel of an established resort property, part new construction. The existing resort is being brought fully up to premium standard, while several new buildings are being added to the site, expanding the resort's footprint and unit count. For investors, this approach offers the best of both: the infrastructure and cost advantages of an existing property, combined with the yield potential of a purpose-designed new development.

05  /  08

Pizza Restaurant

An on-site pizzeria that solves the question every family asks on night one: where do we eat? Wood-fired pies, casual atmosphere, and poolside delivery options. It's a convenience guests value immediately — and mention in every review they leave.

06  /  08

Pickleball Courts

Two regulation courts set beneath shade structures with quality court surfacing. Pickleball is the fastest-growing sport in America — and an increasingly common reason guests specifically seek out resort properties. Equipment provided on request.

07  /  08

Rooftop Terraces

An elevated bar and lounge experience set above the resort, with open sky and views across the Orlando horizon. Signature cocktails, curated spirits, and the kind of atmosphere that turns a holiday into a memory. For investors, a rooftop bar is a revenue line in its own right — and the detail that consistently elevates a property from well-reviewed to genuinely sought-after.

08  /  08

Fitness Centre

A well-equipped fitness studio with cardio machines, free weights, and dedicated stretching space. For longer-stay guests and working professionals — an increasingly large segment of the Orlando STR market — a quality gym is no longer optional. It's expected.
